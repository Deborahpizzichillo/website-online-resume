Asinus XX originated in 2007 by Deborah Pizzichillo Crist' Carlon'. Asinus XX started off as Deborah Pizzichillo Crist' Carlon's artist 'Tag' and is going to evolve into a lifestyle brand. Asinus XX is going to open up a shop and is going to sell some designs and merch items. Asinus XX is a lifestyle brand that is backed with inspiration from classical art, street art and adventure with the hopes of spreading positive vibes. Here at Asinus XX we push equality and hopefully inspire people to Follow Their Bliss.
