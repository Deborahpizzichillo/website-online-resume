About

From an early age I have been passionate about technology,architecture,art and fashion. Here I am! A creative centipede with a great sense of responsibility and a healthy dose of ambition. Customer-friendliness and a good service are qualities that make sense to me. Furthermore, I am someone who attaches great importance to accuracy and I love to challenge myself and keep raising the bar. I am looking for an exciting, tech and creative challenge in which I want to commit myself 100% every day.

I am currently in a work environment / study in which we are trained as a Web Developer. Our training uses a unique approach, with a strong focus on self-learning. The advantage of companies is that employees / students of Becode not only learn programming languages, but also learn how to solve problems through cooperation.

People in my area describe me as a very creative person who likes to work together, listens well, works hard and likes to finish things well.

I'm looking for an interesting workplace, that will give my ambition a space to grow. My plans for the future are to become a full-fledged full stack web developer."
